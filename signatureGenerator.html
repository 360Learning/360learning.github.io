How to upload your photo

To provide a link to your picture on the signature generator, you need to host it on 360learning! Here's how:
PS: You can skip the first two steps if you already have a photo in your 360Learning profile.

  1. First, connect to your account on 360Learning plateform.
  2. Then go to your settings and add a photo to your profile.
  3. Then go to your profile page.
  4. And finally right click on your awesome photo and click "copy the address of the image".

You just have to paste that link in the "Photo Link" field and voila!


Copy your signature

Once everything's done, click on the big button to copy your signature into your clipboard. Then, you can paste it on Gmail!
Don't try to copy it by selecting it, or it may break it.
