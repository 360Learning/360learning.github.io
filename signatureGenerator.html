How to upload your photo

To provide a link to your picture on the signature generator, you need to host it on the web! Here's how:

  1. First upload your picture (when it's ready) to Google drive.
  2. Then in the sharing settings, make it public.
  3. Then go to GDURL to get a direct link.

That's the link you can paste in the signature generator! If you need help, it's all explained in this video tutorial.


Copy your signature

Once everything's done, click on the big button to copy your signature into your clipboard. Then, you can paste it on Gmail!
Don't try to copy it by selecting it, or it may break it.
